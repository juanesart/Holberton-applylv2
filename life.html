How is life with diabetes?

Treatment

Although the type of treatment is subject to the type of diabetes, they all share certain fundamental aspects such as:

  • Constantly monitor the level of sugar in the blood.
  • Have a record of relevant events.
  • Always take medications and doses recommended by your doctor.
  • Have a balanced diet with the help of a nutritionist.
  • Exercise at least three times a week for at least an hour.
  • Do frequent check-ups with your doctor in charge.

Personal experience

My name is Juan, I am 22 years old, I was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes ten years ago, really at that time my life took a 180 degree turn, it was a strong blow that would change my life and that of my family. it was not easy to accept much less incorporate all the changes in my life, however, thanks to the help of my family and a little effort on my part, this is nothing more than a simple anecdote.

To this day I can assure that with the necessary care and discipline, every diabetic person can have a good quality of life, after all this, more than a disease, it is a lifestyle that consists of exercise, diet, medications , records and most importantly education.
